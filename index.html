WORSHIP

Is it something that has to be obvious in my life? What role does it have in my relationship with God, and why? Is there a formula for me to stick too, and if so, what is it? What does the Word say about worship and how relevant is it to me today?

Living

My life is his creation. He is worthy to be praised. David describes God in Psalm 18 as a God who absolutely has his back. David poured a great deal of energy into his worship of God, and in spite of ongoing drama and conflict during his whole life, was inspired enough to write a good chunk of Psalms that we sing today.

Praising

Let everything that has breath, Praise The Lord! (Psalm 150) Why? Phillipians 4:8 lists the things we should meditate on. These things actually describe the very features of our God! (Among many others) Just, pure, lovely, of good report, worthy of praise.

Being

Being with each other, together with God. Ephesians 4 describes our God who is above us all, through us all and in us all. We are united by the Spirit in sync with each other and with God. We may have differences of opinion, interpretations, backgrounds, and interests, but when we worship together we are all on the same page. God Is Good!
